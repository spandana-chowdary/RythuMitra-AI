# RythuMitra AI

AI Voice-First Farming Companion for Indian Farmers.

## Problem Statement

Indian farmers face challenges such as:
- Weather uncertainty
- Crop diseases
- Market price fluctuations
- Lack of awareness of government schemes
- Difficulty using text-heavy applications

## Solution

RythuMitra AI enables farmers to simply speak in Telugu and receive AI-powered farming assistance.

## Features

- Voice-first Telugu AI assistant
- Crop disease detection
- Weather intelligence
- Harvest recommendations
- Medicine suggestions
- Market prices
- Government schemes assistance
- Farmer community
